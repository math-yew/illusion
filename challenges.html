Fun Illusion Challenges

Try these simple challenges and see how your brain processes illusions!

1. The Moving Dots Illusion

Stare at a fixed point in a pattern of dots. Do they seem to move?

2. The Color Change Illusion

Stare at a colored square for 30 seconds, then look at a white surface. What do you see?

3. The Vanishing Object Test

Focus on an object in your peripheral vision for long enough, and it may disappear.
