How Optical Illusions Work

Optical illusions occur due to the way our brain processes information. Here are some key reasons:

1. The Brain Fills in Gaps

Our visual system fills in missing details based on experience, leading to illusions.

2. Contrast and Shadows Affect Perception

Shadows and surrounding colors influence how we perceive shades and depth.

3. Motion and Perspective Tricks

Our brain uses motion cues and depth perception, which can sometimes be misleading.
